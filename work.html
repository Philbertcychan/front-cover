Grocery Price Pressure Dashboard 2026

Canadian grocery CPI tracked across 8 categories (Statistics Canada, Jan 2021 – Apr 2026), with category-specific Canadian food-demand elasticity estimates applied to model a low/base/high range of demand sensitivity. The standout: meat is up 7.4% YoY, implying a ~6.9% demand pullback at the base-case elasticity. Built as a self-directed project alongside an application to Instacart's Strategic Finance team.

SaaS Unit Economics Simulator 2026

Interactive model for exploring how CAC, churn, ARPU, gross margin, and NDR interact to shape LTV, payback period, and long-run revenue. Single-cohort payback analysis with a zero-crossing annotation, a churn sensitivity table, a scenario toggle, and a multi-cohort compounding view showing how revenue builds as overlapping cohorts layer over 24 months. NDR models expansion revenue compounding monthly on surviving customers.
